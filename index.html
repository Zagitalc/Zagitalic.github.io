Causes of climate Change

Human activities: Burning coal oil to generate electricity

Greenhouse gas such as CO2 and methane are produced from Fuel Combustion to the atmosphere

Human activities: Human Deforestation

cutting down of trees can relase up to 1/5 of CO2 gas to the atmosphere, contributing to global warming

Human activities: Overconsumption of meat

Thanks to the special digestive system of cow and sheep, a greenhouse gas methane is released which has 30 times the amount of CO2.

Naturally accured causes:
  • Leakage of greenhouse gas from the Permafrost(frozen soil).

    • Permafrost in the Earth's crust accounts of up to 850 gigatons of Carbon

    Impacts of climate Change

    Typical impacts might lead to rise in CO2 level, which might lead to global warming. As a result, rising temperature give rise to extreme weathers. One of which is the formation of Hurricanes or Typhoons.

    Examples and Datasets

    Researches have shown that the wind intensity , rainfall, and frequency of occurence of Tropical cyclones have positive corelation with increasing temperature caused by global warming. Study from The Nature has also shown that the endurhance of hurricane has increased up to 20% from the 1960s, meaning an increase of longetivity and destruction to citizens of affected areas.
