Causes of climate Change



Human activities: Burning coal oil to generate electricity

Greenhouse gas such as CO2 and methane are produced from Fuel Combustion to the atmosphere

Human activities: Human Deforestation

Cutting down of trees can relase up to 1/5 of CO2 gas to the atmosphere, contributing to global warming

Human activities: Overconsumption of meat

Thanks to the special digestive system of cow and sheep, a greenhouse gas methane is released which has 30 times the amount of CO2.

Naturally accured causes:
  • Leakage of greenhouse gas from the Permafrost(frozen soil).

    • Permafrost in the Earth's crust accounts of up to 850 gigatons of Carbon

    Evidence of climate Change: Tropical cyclones

    Human activities and natural carbon emisstions give rise to greenhouse gases such as CO2, Chlorofluorocarbons, and Methane, concentrated in the atmosphere, which might lead to global warming. As a result, rising temperature give rise to extreme weathers. One of which is the formation of Hurricanes or Typhoons.

    Intensity of hurricanes

    Study from The Nature has also shown that the endurance of hurricane has increased up to 20% from the 1960s, meaning an increase of longetivity and destruction to citizens of affected areas.

    Precipitation of hurricanes

    Study from The GDFL(Global Warming and Hurricanes) has also shown that the amount of precipitation has increased of 10-15% amist 2 degree Celsius of ocean temperature increase

    frequency of hurricanes

    Study from PNAS has also shown that since the past 40 years, the frequency of hurricanes has an annual increase of up to 49% in the North Atlantic, Year 2020 is also the year of the most Atlantic hurricanes with up to 30 storms
